FIDGETY DOG COFFEE SHOP
About the Shop

Fidgety Dog opened in 2014 and features fair trade coffee and teas specialties as well as fresh baked goods. It is a pet-friendly establishment with homemade treats for your four-legged friends. It is located in historic Highland Square in Akron

ABOUT THE OWNER

Amy is an avid coffee drinker who decided life would be better constantly surrounded by it. She's also an avid dog lover who donates part of the proceeds of her sales to animal rescue groups
